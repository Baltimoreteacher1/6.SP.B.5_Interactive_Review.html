Grade 6 Math Review — Standard 6.SP.B.5

Summarizing and describing distributions

Key Vocabulary

  • Distribution — how data values are spread out
  • Mean — the average of the data
  • Median — the middle value when data are ordered
  • Interquartile Range (IQR) — distance between Q1 and Q3
  • Mean Absolute Deviation (MAD) — average distance from the mean
